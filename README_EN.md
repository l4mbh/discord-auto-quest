# Discord Quest Manager

A powerful, automated tool designed to help you complete Discord Quests effortlessly. Manage multiple accounts, run quests in the background, and track your progress with ease.

## ‚ú® Key Features

-   **Automated Quest Completion**: Simulates streaming and playing activities to complete quest requirements automatically.
-   **Multi-Account Support**: Manage and switch between multiple Discord accounts seamlessly.
-   **Background Operation**: Runs minimized in the system tray so you can continue using your PC without interruption.
-   **Orb Balance Tracking**: View your current Orb balance directly in the app. Updates in real-time.
-   **Rich Notifications**: Get standard Windows notifications when quests are completed or rewards are ready.
-   **Safety First**: Uses standard Discord protocols to ensure account safety. Clear Cache option available for troubleshooting.

## üöÄ How to Use

1.  **Add Account**: Click the `+` button and enter your Discord Token.
2.  **Select Quests**: Browse the "Available" tab and accept the quests you want to do.
3.  **Start Automating**: Select accepted quests and click **"Start Selected"**. The app will simulate the required game activity. Or you can select **"Start All"** to run all quests.
4.  **Track Progress**: Watch the progress bars fill up. You'll get a notification when a quest is done.

## ‚ö†Ô∏è Important: Claiming Rewards

**Please Read Carefully:**

Due to the complex and constantly changing nature of Discord's Captcha security systems, **automated claiming is currently not possible**. The "Captcha" challenges are extremely difficult for bots to solve reliably.

**How to Claim:**
We have integrated a secure **Manual Claim Window** to resolve this:

1.  Go to the **"Completed"** tab in the app.
2.  Click the **"Claim in Discord"** button.
3.  A secure, in-app browser window will open.
    -   *The app will automatically log you into your current account.*
4.  Navigate to the Discord Quest/Inventory page in that window.
5.  **Manually click the "Claim Reward" button.**
6.  Once done, close the window to return to the app.

This method ensures you can always claim your rewards safely without getting stuck on impossible Captchas.
